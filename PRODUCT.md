# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small business owners, startup founders, agency partners, and consultants who need a fast, trustworthy website or workflow automation. They are technically-savvy enough to know what they want but too busy to chase agencies. They land on the site evaluating fit — looking for substance, not flash.

## Product Purpose

KritRaj Nexera is a boutique digital agency that sells two services: custom website development and n8n workflow automation. The website's job is to communicate expertise, build trust quickly, and convert visitors into inbound leads or booked calls — without overselling or feeling like a template.

## Brand Personality

Precise, Confident, Direct. The site speaks in plain language, avoids jargon, and never hypes. It feels like talking to a senior developer who tells you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear.

## Anti-references

Anything that feels templated, generic, or mass-produced. No Wix/Squarespace energy. No stock photography of people shaking hands. No over-designed animations or gradients that signal "agency" instead of "craft." The site should feel hand-built, which it literally is.

## Design Principles

- **Substance over style.** Every visual decision serves clarity and trust. Decorative elements earn their place.
- **Same-day response, same-day tone.** The site's voice matches its service: fast, honest, no runaround.
- **One standard.** Both services (websites and automation) get the same level of craft. The design reinforces that parity.
- **Built to be read.** Typography, contrast, and spacing prioritize readability over trend-following.
- **Respect the visitor's time.** Clear structure, scannable sections, no fluff. The visitor should know what KritRaj Nexera does within 5 seconds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Targets WCAG AA. Skip link, semantic landmarks, focus-visible outlines, `prefers-reduced-motion` support, and proper aria attributes are all present. Body text contrast must stay above 4.5:1.
